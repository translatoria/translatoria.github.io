Translation


Translating is not just transcribing the literal meaning of a text's words into another language. It's not a matter of words. It's a matter of cultures. Blue Diamond wants to make sure your text reaches your intended audience smoothly and effectively. Our translators need to convey implied meaning, tone, humor, culture, and more from one language to another. This is why we study your text, your company, and the purpose of your text.

The content we work with includes contracts, manuals, training materials, news articles, textbooks, surveys, forms, reports, business proposals, product information, safety guides, and social media content, for example. We offer Translation Solutions to the Fashion Industry, Beauty Industry, Tourism Industry, and Food & Beverages Industry.

Subtitles are a way of communicating with other cultures, promoting accessibility to the deaf and hard of hearing, and helping your audience to process what is being said in your video. Since over 28 million people play videos without turning the sound on, having your video subtitled delivers your message to more people. Besides all that, subtitles also improve Search Engine Optimization (SEO) on Youtube, Instagram, and Facebook.

Blue Diamond Translation Agency can transcribe, translate, and add subtitles to a variety of your content. We work with online courses, business presentations, corporate videos, educational videos, training videos, interviews, music videos, ads, movies, TV Shows, promo videos, and many more.

Transcreation recreates your source materials in other cultures taking idioms, slang, references, and cultural elements into account to sound idiomatic. Successfully transcreated content speaks to your audience. It makes them feel the same emotions as the original intended.

Colors, feelings, and meanings can change from one culture to another. We translate the text and transcreate the taglines, puns, images, colors, and even your company's logo.
